Facts about Dogs

Dogs are domesticated carnivores belonging to the family of Canidae. They belong to the mammal category as female dogs can give birth to puppies.

They also have mammary glands, and nourish the puppies with milk. Dogs are known to be good swimmers and also friendly and helpful to humans. Dogs were the first species to be domesticated by humans over 15,000 years before the development of agriculture.
